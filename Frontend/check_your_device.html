IOS Apple

Find It Within Your
Device

  • From Your Home Screen, Select "Settings"
  • Select "General"
  • Select "About"
  • Scroll Down To The IMEI, ESN Or MEID Number

Android

Option 1 - Find It Within Your Device

  • From Your Home Screen, Select "Menu".
  • Select "Settings"
  • Select "About Phone"
  • Select "Status"
  • Scroll Down To The IMEI, ESN Or MEID Number

Option 2 - Find It On The Outside Of Your Device

  • Remove The Battery From Your Device.
  • Locate The IMEI, ESN Or MEID Number On The Inside Of Your Device. See The Image For Reference.

Windows Phone

Option 1 - Find It Within Your Device

  • From Start Screen, Select The Right Arrow (Located In The Upper-Right).
  • Select "Settings"
  • Select "About Phone"
  • Select 'More Info' And The MEID Number Will Appear.

Option 2 - Find It On The Outside Of Your Device

  • Remove The Battery From Your Device.
  • Locate The IMEI, ESN Or MEID Number On The Inside Of Your Device. See The Image For Reference.

Basic Phone

Option 1 - Find It On The Outside Of Your Device

  • Remove The Battery From Your Device.
  • Locate The IMEI, ESN, Or MEID Number On The Inside Of Your Device. See Image For Reference.

Option 2 - Find It On Your Device’s Packaging

  • Check The Inside And Outside Of The Box Your Device Came In For The MEID, ESN Or IMEI.

Option 3 - Find It On Your Device’s Receipt

  • Review The Device’s Purchase Receipt For A Number Labeled MEID, ESN Or IMEI.
